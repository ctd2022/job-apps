üìä Understanding Your ATS Report

Score Ranges:

  • 80-100%: Excellent! Strong keyword match - high likelihood of passing ATS
  • 60-79%: Good coverage, but consider adding missing keywords
  • Below 60%: Needs significant optimization to pass ATS screening

What's Included:

  • Overall ATS match percentage
  • Key requirements (hard skills, soft skills, qualifications)
  • Top keywords from job description
  • Missing keywords you should consider adding
  • Specific recommendations for improvement
  • ATS-friendly formatting checklist
